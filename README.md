## 📊 Dashboard Sections

| Tab | Analysis |
|-----|----------|
| 🎯 **Channel Attribution** | Multi-touch attribution models (First Touch, Last Touch, Linear, Time Decay, Data-Driven) + CAC vs Revenue efficiency |
| 📉 **Funnel Analysis** | Full funnel from Sessions→Leads→MQL→SQL→Opps→Won with drop-off heatmap |
| 📅 **Cohort Retention** | Weekly retention heatmap + retention curves for 8 customer cohorts |
| 📈 **Monthly Trends** | Channel-level monthly performance (sessions, leads, revenue) |
| 🏆 **Campaigns** | Campaign ROI matrix + performance scorecard |

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

- **Email converts 3.2× better** than Paid Search (CVR 2.50% vs 0.78%) despite only 6.6% of sessions
- **MQL→SQL is the critical bottleneck** — 49.5% drop-off signals ICP misalignment between Marketing and Sales
- **Week 1 retention is the make-or-break moment** — users who survive W1→W4 show 3× higher LTV
- **Email Nurture ROI: 84,050%** — highest efficiency channel in the entire portfolio
- **Recommendation:** Reallocate 20% of Google PMax budget → LinkedIn ABM (619% vs 478% ROI)
